Dear God,<br>
<br>
AAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH<br>
<br>
Why did You give me love? Why? Why? Why? Why?<br>
I was alright, my cycle worked fine. It was easy.<br>
Why? Why did You let me experience love? Why?<br>
I hate waking up in the middle of my sleep just to cry, feel heaviness on the chest, get headache, unable to go back sleep easily.<br>
<br>
Dear God, I hate this.<br>
I just want to cry because my juniors keep using the glovebox incorrectly that the oxygen level is rising like a stock market.<br>
I just want to cry because of that, but whenever I cry, I remember him.<br>
I already promised myself to not write anything mentioning him again. But oh dear God.<br>
<br>
AAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH<br>
<br>
Dear God, are You telling me that *this* is blessing?<br>
Because it feels like torture.<br>
*I know this is because I am in my period and my period brain keeps getting agitated.*<br>
*I am not stupid. I know that this is good for me, but oh dear God.*<br>
<br>
AAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH<br>
<br>
Dear God, let me just cite Taylor's *I Can Do It With a Broken Heart*.<br>
Let me hate him just for today.<br>
<br>
