It is past midnight, I came back from a startup event, it was a highlight of my week. I have been in bed for the past few days due to a menstrual cramp, doing nothing except enduring the pain, waking up at 3 am because the pain was unbearable.<br>
<br>
I opened my old writing from July last year. I was so mad at you, because of your letter. I didn't expect your confession would disappoint me that much, but it did. And somehow, along the way, I still don't understand why Allah places this feelings into my heart. I don't understand why He places the peace whenever I choose to be honest with you. I don't understand why He keeps giving you second chances, when I gave up.<br>
<br>
He loves you.<br>
He knows I love Him more than I love you.<br>
He knows I will show His love to you.<br>
Although I don't know whether you can see it through your heart.<br>
<br>
If only you know, I didn't love you this much a year ago, two years ago, five years ago. I only knew it three weeks ago. And believe me, half of it doesn't even come from my own will. And I'm grateful for that.<br>
<br>
Under this bright moonlight.<br>
I love you, enough to let you go.<br>
I love you, that I could marry you tomorrow.<br>
I love you, that I wish He will take you from my life.<br>
I love you, my love, that I wish you can love Him as much as I do.<br>
