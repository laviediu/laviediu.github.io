Hi there, I hope you are doing well. Sometimes I wonder whether I have caused too much distractions for you. I hope I am not.<br>
<br>
Do you know that you are probably the first man I respect this much? I saw you, noticed you, and paid attention. It is why I said that I believe in you.<br>
<br>
I choose you, not over others, but over choosing to live without you. I choose you because I want to share my life with you. I choose you over self-companionship that I used to love so much. I guess I love you more.<br>
<br>
I said I wasn't sure that I want to become a mother someday. With you, I could see those future. The dream stays with me now. What I am trying to say is that I can see your goodness and our partnership to care for someone else.<br>
<br>
I love you enough to let you go, not because I do not love you, precisely because I do. I do not want you to resent me by staying with me. I want you to walk your path without the burden of something else. I want you to be honest with yourself. I want you to be the person you want to be. And I hope that person is still someone I see, someone who is wonderfully you.<br>
<br>
There are bad, very bad, voices that can echo in our head. I hear them too, sometimes. Do you know what I will say to push them away? *"Remember why you started, prove that you are not those voices"*. I don't know what kind of words that can help you, but I hope you find yours and use them whenever the voices become loud.<br>
<br>
I hope you can see another angle when things seem to not be in your favor. I hope you are not afraid of falling today, because you can always get up tomorrow. I hope you continue moving forward. It does not matter whether you walk or run, it matters that you keep going forward, not backward.<br>
<br>
Uncertainties are very inconvenient, aren't they? I know, I find them quite troublesome too sometimes. I hope that you can live just for today and let the uncertainties become another day for tomorrow.<br>
<br>
The world has asked a lot from you, hasn't it? You are very strong for standing right where you are, with what you have, and still keep going. I am very proud of you. Not only during the good days, but also during the bad ones. Do not let the bad days define what the future holds.<br>
<br>
I should have talked to you about it more, but I hope you do not keep your longing by yourself. I hope you can talk to your friends the way you talked about it with me. Of course, I would want to be there for you too.<br>
<br>
I love you, choose you, believe in you. I respect your decision, but you can not tell me what to do. I am my own person.<br>
<br>
Now, I am still disappointed that you let the man I love become small, when he is not, at all. *Do not do that to my man. My man does not make himself small.*<br>
<br>
My man will also not let a woman affects his responsibility to care for himself, to do the best for himself, and to live according to his values.<br>
<br>
I do not know whether we will ever meet again, but I hope that when we do, you are still someone that I know.
