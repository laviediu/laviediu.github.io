I didn't sleep last night. I spoke too soon, I cried again, though my chest wasn't heavy anymore. I am very tired, but I can't sleep. It reminds me of that day. I miss him, so bad. I am crying again, oh come on now. I am wondering if he is still the same person who loved me back then. It is very strange that he keeps in contact with another person he had feelings with, but he cuts it off with me. I didn't even ask for a reciprocation. I want to let him go too. I have my closure as well. I can't walk the same path with him, and I accept that. Why is he being so hard on me?<br>
<br>
I just arrived in Seoul, I am very sleep deprived but I can't think of anything except him. This is scary. I tried to close my eyes while I was in the plane and on the bus, yet I kept thinking of him. Why is this still happening, it has been a month. The same way I went to his place, I feel pathetic, again. Why does this keep happening? Why does Allah let this encounter happen? Why did he love me? Does my presence even matter in his life? I feel pathetic, again. I hope I can deliver my presentation tomorrow well, I am grateful that I didn't get the oral slot, I would be damned. Oh God, why do I love him this deeply? What did I do? Why did I suppress my feelings? Why did I carry my parents' problems? O Allah, what should I do now? I do not want to disappear into thin air now, but I am tired. I am very very tired. And I love him. This is scary.
