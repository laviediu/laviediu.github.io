Hi, keep going, you haven't found me!<br>
<br>
I wonder what you are doing at this very second. I am writing a letter to you, isn't that hilarious? I have always enjoyed writing, I have written a lot of letters and poems for you. I hope you find it charming. I also have written a lot to the people who loved me and the people whom I loved. But hey, you are the one who gets to keep me!<br>
<br>
I learned a lot of things about relationship and love recently. I think I have healed my past wounds progressively as well. Look at that, you will meet the better version of me! And I will meet the better version of you too. If, at this very second, you are struggling to see a hope, there are prayers I send to the sky for you. Thank you for sending your prayers to me during the difficult times I have.<br>
<br>
When you finally meet me, I need you to understand several things. I am not perfect either, we are both imperfect people walking towards the same destination while laughing and crying, and it is all we need. I have a big world that occupies my life before we meet, I want you to be a part of it and I want to be a part of yours too, together we will have an even bigger world. Like you, I have a history that shapes how I respond to several things, I hope that you can differentiate between my fears and my trust for you, and I hope you will tell me yours so that I can do the same.<br>
<br>
It takes a very long time, isn't it? I know, I also want to meet you sooner. However, we both know that we won't be where we are now if we meet even a day sooner. I hope you will celebrate how much I have grown as a person when I meet you, because I will celebrate your growth as well. I hope you will not make me apologize for taking a very long time to be the woman of your dream, because I will not make you apologize too.<br>
<br>
Thank you for walking from the other side, for taking the mindful steps, for hoping through the uncertainties, for becoming a better person, for loving me without knowing who I am. I am doing that for myself and for you too. See you then, my love.<br>
<br>
Oh, and when we finally meet, let's hike and camp to see the stars together! Until then, I will hike with my best friend.
