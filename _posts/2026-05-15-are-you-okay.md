Hi, how are you?<br>
<br>
Do you remember that evening when we called, and the first thing you you said was, "are you okay?". It feels unreal sometimes. It was something I had wanted to do four years ago, when I cried by myself in the bedroom while looking at your texts, holding myself back as much as I could to not hit the call button.<br>
<br>
I wish I could have been that person for you as well. I wish you can tell me when it gets too hard. I wish there is an easier way to do this.<br>
<br>
There was a 6.7-scale earthquake today, it should have been near to your place. You let me know whether you were alright, but I can see how this becomes harder especially now that everything is out. That we try as much as we can to respect the boundaries, to see how we are doing when we are apart. It hasn't been easy for me, and I bet it has been harder for you.<br>
<br>
So, tonight, I want to be that person, courageous enough to write it here, though not to you directly.<br>
<br>
*Are you okay?*<br>
