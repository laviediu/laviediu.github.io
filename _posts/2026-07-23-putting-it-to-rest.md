Where do I have to put this feeling to rest? I can't call my mom as I would hate to see her seeing me crying. I can't cry in my room because it would be too intimate for my neighbor to listen. I can't cry in front of you because you left. I can only cry to God but I can't even recite the Qur'an and pray because I am a woman. I respect your decision, I do, I am letting you go, but my tears can't stop falling and I don't know how to put this to rest. I don't even see you anymore, I don't talk about you, I ask God to take you from me, but you are still here, in my heart. I can't even hate you for more than 12 hours. I didn't bring you peace, I hurt you, I made you feel worthless, I don't even deserve you, but I still don't know how to put this to rest. I have work, I am working, damn I am writing this in front of the glovebox while sobbing, but I still don't know how to put this to rest. How could you ask me to put it to rest just like that. How could you leave like that. How is it so easy for you.
