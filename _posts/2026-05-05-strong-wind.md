The sky is blue,<br>
the wind is so strong, <br>
that it reminds me of that day I met you.<br>
<br>
The wind has been like this<br>
for the past few days.<br>
<br>
As if the thought of you<br>
isn't enough to fill my head,<br>
my body has to endure<br>
the cold breeze<br>
whenever I'm outside.<br>
