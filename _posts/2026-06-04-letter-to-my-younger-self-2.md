It has been almost a year, hello again, sweetheart. How are you? I bet you already aced that economics exam, yeah? Excellent! Hey, this means you're now in the second year and you finally wear the hijab! A good decision sweetie, you followed your heart. I know it was because your friends were commenting on how your new haircut makes you look different, prettier. Instead of liking those comments, you were bothered haha. And when you told Mom about it, she immediately agreed to your decision. Mom is the best, isn't she?<br>
<br>
Actually, today, I finally told Mom about us crying every day for the past week. I told her that it is very hard to be steadfast. She understands it. She asked whether I can call her if I am not busy. Well, sweetheart, we have been crying *while* synthesizing samples. It is a good thing that I am working in a glovebox. No, I won't ruin the samples because of the tears haha. Why are we crying? <br>
<br>
Pumpkin, you will love, deeply, sincerely. This is the story where you let the love gets reciprocated. It is huge, I know! We are grateful that we get to know him. He is a really good person. I imagined a future with him, some of those images are yours, your dreams. I am grateful that even if we are not together, the blurry person became a man, a man who loves us carefully, patiently.<br>
<br>
Honey, I am almost 27 now. Even before meeting him, I know very well that we may never experience the sweetness of living a life with someone in this world. And we both know that romance is not everything, although you, my princess, is such a romantic queen! Perhaps, Allah may let us enter His Jannah. I know, not anybody can enter that holy place. Oh but do you know who will be there? His mother!!! You have been sending dua to Allah every night, let's not stop it, let's hope that she will be placed in even higher Jannah, because she has been giving us a present. Let us love her from down below on this Earth. Perhaps, Allah will pass our salaam to her through the angels. Allah is merciful like that.<br>
<br>
Pumpkin, do not be sad, we love him. I know it is hard, but we love him, and we are grateful for the love that has been placed in our heart. Allah knows very well how we feel. Do not be hard on yourself. It is okay to say that you want him. It is okay to feel that you love him. Allah does not ask you to bury the feelings. He asks you to be mindful of Him. Also, do you know that there is a concept called Kalam theological argument? He introduced it to us! I watched some youtube videos from Basira Education, and their contents are super cool! I want to learn more about it. We should!<br>
<br>
Sweetheart, thank you for not giving up on us. Someday, when that day comes, when Mom or Dad returns to Allah's side, do not be by yourself. Do not lock your door. Do not go to the sea at night. Do not have a scary thought. Call your best friends. Be with the people who love you. <br>
<br>
Sweetie, you have been growing a lot, I love you. Don't forget to kiss Mom and Dad's hands again tomorrow! I am waiting for subuh, it is summertime again, and I am battling with this ridiculous four season country timezone haha. I will sleep after you. See you again next year when you graduate from middle school!
