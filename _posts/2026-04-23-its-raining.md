It is raining in Tokyo. On the way home, I let the umbrella tilted so that the raindrops reached my tired eyes. There was nothing on the sky, yet it was glimmering, even when the moon disappeared. The flourescent street light hit the wet leaves, they looked so alive, that I almost get jealous.
