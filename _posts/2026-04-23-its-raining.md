It is raining in Tokyo. On the way home, I let the umbrella tilted so that the raindrops reached my tired eyes. There was nothing on the sky, yet it was glimmering, even when the moon disappeared. The flourescent street light hit the wet leaves, they looked so alive, that I almost get jealous.<br>
<br>
It has been days, I haven't heard from you. I keep asking the not-so-smart AI about what you feel while reading the long letter. It was honest, extremely honest, that I sigh a lot.<br>
<br>
I have lost in words, in translation, in the dictionary that we found.<br>
<br>
I wish you well.
