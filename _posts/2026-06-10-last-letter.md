I did not feel the heaviness on my chest this morning, it was gone, just like that. How could it be true? I still love you, very much. Perhaps, because I already said everything left to say.<br>
<br>
I re-opened your last text, then realizing how exceptionally delusional I was in my writings. I then cried, hard, I mean the kind that my voice was cracking and I couldn't even stand up or sit down, so I kneeled. I always kneeled. It happened in the lab, when everyone was already home. After I contained myself, I went home with puffy eyes. I felt better. Then I read the Quran, it was surah Muhammad. The name of The Prophet already brought relief somehow. However, this time, I couldn't feel the peace while reading it, my mind was somewhere else. I was in the verge of contacting you. I already wrote the texts and sent it in my personal chatbox. It wasn't something you could answer, so I decided not to send it.<br>
<br>
I miss you, I want to keep writing to you from this blog, even though I know no one reads it. I need the feelings somewhere to go. And yet, what if this means I am reliving our connection?<br>
<br>
I do not need to heal and move on, in a sense that it is what I should do, I don't want to keep pushing the feelings into memories. I want to hold this love and let it slide naturally. It is already a gift, a Black Swan, that it happens at all. I have never let myself to feel this being reciprocated. This is my first real love, and a very long connection for that matter. There is nothing to forgive because I am truly grateful that it is you. You do not stay for just a while in my story. Even though, that is how you feel. Every time you appear, it is one connection. Perhaps, that's why I feel pathetic. Because I put too much meaning into things. I hold on to hope. And yet, what does it mean if I lose all my hopes? I would see this world as a land of suffering, this life as nothing but suffering.<br>
<br>
Do I want us to be together? Of course I do. Do I want you to be happy? Of course I do, I love you. However, can you be happy with me? I do not know that.<br>
<br>
I know you will never read this, but I want to keep a record that *you* matter enormously to me. This might be the last time I write to you. Honestly, I still enjoy it very much, I wish I could share how my morning and afternoon went today. And yet, am I reopening wounds by doing that?<br>
<br>
Why is this life so hard? Do you really believe that this life is all there is?
